Brief Itinerary

Day 1
Reach Shimla- Transfer to Kalpa via Kinnaur Gate.
Day 2
Into the Heart of Spiti – Kalpa to Tabo via Nako.
Day 3
Spiritual Heights – Dhankar, Key Monastery & Chicham Bridge.
Day 4
Village Vibes – Hikkim, Komic & Langza.
Day 5
Waterfall Wonders – Kaza to Kalpa via Lingti.
Day 6
Borderline Beauty – Kalpa to Chitkul.
Day 7
Depart to Shimla. Overnight transfer to Delhi.

Key Attractions

  • Cross the mighty Kunzum La (4,551m) and begin your winter odyssey into Spiti's icy landscapes.
  • Spend time in Kalpa, with breathtaking views of the majestic Kinner Kailash range.
  • Visit the serene Nako Lake village and witness its frozen beauty in winter.
  • Explore the ancient Tabo Monastery, the "Ajanta of the Himalayas," steeped in history and culture.
  • Marvel at Dhankar Monastery, perched dramatically above the confluence of rivers.
  • Visit the iconic Key Monastery, a beacon of peace and history in Spiti.
  • Walk across Chicham Bridge, one of the highest suspension bridges in Asia.
  • Send Postcard to loved ones from Hikkim – home to the world's highest post office.
  • Visit Komic, one of the world's highest motorable villages.
  • Spot the giant statue of Buddha and marine fossils at Langza Village.
  • Capture the beauty of Lingti Waterfall en route back to Kalpa.
  • Explore Chitkul, the last inhabited Indian village near the Indo-Tibet border.
  • Enjoy one of India's most thrilling winter road trips on rugged Himalayan roads with like-minded explorers.
